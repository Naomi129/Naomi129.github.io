Spread Information

Three-Card Spread

This is a basic and practical spread that examines your question through three time dimensions:

  • Card 1: Represents the past influence
  • Card 2: Reflects the current situation
  • Card 3: Predicts the future development

Suitable for: Daily inquiries, simple questions, timeline analysis

Crossroads Spread

This spread is designed for decision-making and choices. It contains four positions:

  • Center: Your current situation
  • Left: The first option/choice
  • Right: The second option/choice
  • Bottom: The potential outcome

Suitable for: Facing a choice, decision analysis, comparing pros and cons

Celtic Cross Spread

The most comprehensive and in-depth spread. It uses ten positions to provide a detailed analysis:

  • Center: The core issue
  • Cross: The immediate challenge
  • Base: The past foundation
  • Influences: Surrounding influences
  • Goal: The desired outcome
  • Future: Potential developments

Suitable for: Complex issues, deep analysis, comprehensive understanding
