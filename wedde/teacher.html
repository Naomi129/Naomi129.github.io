Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible for the teacher discount?
Teachers and educational staff, including professors, instructors, and administrators, are eligible for this discount.
What documents are accepted for verification?
Accepted documents include a teacher ID, employment letter, or a faculty ID from an accredited institution.
How long does it take to verify my ID?
Verification typically takes 1-2 business days. You will be notified via email once the process is complete.
How do I use my discount?
After verification, you'll receive a unique discount code to use at checkout. Enter the code to receive your discount.
