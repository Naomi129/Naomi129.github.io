Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible for the military discount?
Current military personnel, veterans, and immediate family members are eligible for this discount.
What documents are accepted for verification?
Accepted documents include a military ID, DD-214 form, or a VA-issued card.
How long does it take to verify my ID?
Verification usually takes 1-2 business days. You will be notified via email once the process is complete.
How do I use my discount?
Once verified, you will receive a unique discount code to use at checkout. Simply enter the code to receive the discount.
