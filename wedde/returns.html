Returns and Exchanges

Return Policy

We accept returns and exchanges within 30 days of purchase. Items must be in their original condition, with tags and packaging intact.

Returns after 30 days are subject to a restocking fee, and refunds will be issued as store credit.

How to Return an Item

  1. Contact our customer support team to obtain a Return Authorization Number (RAN).
  2. Package the item securely, including all original packaging and accessories.
  3. Include a copy of the receipt or order confirmation email in the package.
  4. Ship the item to our returns address using a traceable shipping method.
  5. Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we will process your refund or exchange.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • Q: Who pays for return shipping? A: Customers are responsible for return shipping costs, except for defective or incorrect items.
  • Q: How long does it take to process a return? A: Returns are usually processed within 5-10 business days after receipt.
  • Q: Can I return a customized item? A: Customized or personalized items are non-returnable unless they are defective.
