Shipping and Delivery

Shipping Methods

We offer a variety of shipping methods to suit your needs. Our primary shipping carriers are UPS and FedEx.

You can choose between standard shipping, express shipping, and overnight shipping depending on your location and urgency.

Estimated Delivery Times

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping takes 1-2 business days, and overnight shipping is delivered by the next business day.

Delivery times may vary depending on your location and weather conditions.

Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated based on the weight and dimensions of your order, as well as your location.

We offer free standard shipping for orders over $50. Express and overnight shipping incur additional costs.

Returns and Exchanges

We accept returns and exchanges within 30 days of purchase. To initiate a return, please contact our customer support team.

Returned items must be in their original condition with tags and packaging intact.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • Q: What if my package is delayed? A: If your package is delayed, please contact our support team for assistance.
  • Q: Can I change my shipping address? A: Yes, you can change your shipping address before the order is shipped. Contact our support team to make changes.
  • Q: How can I track my order? A: You can track your order using the tracking number provided in your order confirmation email.
